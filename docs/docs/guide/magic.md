## Magic Overview

Magic in DOKAPON! Sword of Fury is divided into two types:
- **Attack Magic** - Offensive spells that deal damage or inflict effects
- **Defense Magic** - Protective spells that reduce or negate damage

Magic is equipped in dedicated equipment slots and can be used as battle commands.

---

## Attack Magic Mechanics

### Key Points

- Attack Magic uses the **"Attack Magic"** command in battle
- Damage is based on **MG (Magic)** stat unless specified otherwise
- **Elemental weakness** deals **1.3x damage** (same as weapon weakness)
- Attack Magic **always hits** - it ignores enemy SP (Speed)
- Has **limited uses** that are consumed on cast (Remaster has unlimited option)
- Effects only trigger if enemy doesn't use Defense Magic

### Elements

| Element | Japanese | Strong Against |
|---------|----------|----------------|
| Lightning | 雷 | Various monsters |
| Ice | 氷 | Fire-type monsters |
| Fire | 炎 | Ice-type monsters |
| Absorption | 吸収 | Special effects |
| Dark | 闇 | Status effects, instant kill |
| Physical | 物理 | Uses AT instead of MG |

### Physical Attack Magic

These spells use **AT (Attack)** instead of MG for damage:
- **Teleport Slash** (テレポーザン)
- **Clone Slash** (ブンシンザン)

Note: **Sword Dancer** is Physical type but uses MG.

### Magic Shield Counter

If the enemy equips **Magic Shield** (マジックシールド), Attack Magic damage is reduced to **1/4**.

## Magic Strategy Tips

### Attack Magic Tips

1. **Use for guaranteed damage** - Magic always hits, unlike Attack/Deathblow
2. **Target elemental weaknesses** for 1.3x damage bonus
3. **Go To Hell** is expensive but can instantly win battles
4. **Zeni Get spells** are great for gold farming
5. **Mahokes** spells can disable dangerous enemy magic

### Defense Magic Tips

1. **Magic Shield** cuts Attack Magic damage to 1/4
2. **Reflect** can turn powerful spells against the caster
3. Save Defense Magic for dangerous attacks

### Alliance Strategy

1. Coordinate element types with your ally for powerful combos
2. **Giga Drain** can devastate enemies in one hit
3. Alliance magic only works against non-weak monsters
