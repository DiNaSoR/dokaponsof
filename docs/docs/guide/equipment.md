## Equipment Overview

Equipment in DOKAPON! Sword of Fury consists of five slots:
- **Weapon** - Increases AT (Attack)
- **Shield** - Increases DF (Defense)
- **Armor** - Increases DF (Defense)
- **Attack Magic** - Offensive spells
- **Defense Magic** - Defensive spells

{: .note }
Unlike other Dokapon games, you can equip Attack Magic even without a weapon in Sword of Fury.

## Weapon Mechanics

### Cursed Weapons
{: .d-inline-block }

Warning
{: .label .label-red }

Some weapons are **cursed**. Once equipped, you cannot change weapons until you pay to remove the curse at a church.

### Weapon Destruction

Weapons can be destroyed by:
- Monster special skills
- Certain attack magic
- Losing battles
- Field magic like "Barabaramba"

### Special Effects

Many weapons have special effects:
- **Status ailments** - Poison, Sleep, Charm, Curse
- **Elemental damage** - Fire, Ice, Lightning
- **Critical bonuses** - Increased damage chance
- **Gold theft** - Steal money on hit
- **Equipment break** - Destroy enemy armor/shield
